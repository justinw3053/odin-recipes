Pizza Recipe

Pizza on the Grill

Pizza on the grill is a fantastic way to make pizza at home.

The heat of a hot grill is a perfect match for a professional pizza oven. Learn the tricks for making and grilling pizza from scratch with this recipe. Feel free to use your favorite toppings, but keep in mind not to overload the pizza.

Ingredients

  • Pizza Dough
  • Garlic Oil
  • Pizza Toppings and cheese

Let's Make It!

  1. Gather ingredients
  2. Make or buy the dough
  3. Make garlic oil
  4. Grill the dough
  5. Brush the dough with the garlic oil and add toppings and cheese
  6. Serve once cheese melts
